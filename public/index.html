Extended Reality (XR) Applications

Retail & Shopping

Try before you buy with virtual product previews in your space. See furniture in your home or try on clothes and accessories digitally.

Navigation & Travel

Real-time directions overlaid on your view, highlighting points of interest, translating foreign signs, and providing contextual information about your surroundings.

Education & Learning

Interactive 3D models bring concepts to life. Explore human anatomy, chemistry molecules, historical reconstructions, and planetary systems in immersive detail.

Healthcare & Medicine

Visualize patient anatomy, assist in surgical planning, enhance rehabilitation, and provide real-time guidance for medical procedures with augmented overlays.

Gaming & Entertainment

Transform your environment into a playground with AR games that blend digital elements with physical space, creating immersive experiences and social interactions.

Industrial & Manufacturing

Enhance worker productivity with hands-free guided assembly, remote expert assistance, equipment maintenance visualization, and quality control inspections.
