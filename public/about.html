About Us

We are designing and manufacturing an augmented reality headset.

What We Do

ARmed is an engineering team focused on building a fully custom augmented reality headset. We handle the entire development cycle — from PCB design, optical integration, and embedded systems to Unity-based software, real-time visualization, and UI/UX optimization.

Our headset is designed for advanced applications in education and simulation. By bridging hardware and software, we aim to unlock new possibilities in spatial computing and immersive learning. Our work combines mechanical design, sensor integration, and cutting-edge graphics to push the limits of mixed reality technology.

Our Mission Statement

To design, manufacture, and program a next-generation Augmented Reality headset from the ground up. Our goal is to redefine how users interact with digital content in real space — starting with medical education.

We are committed to engineering excellence, iterative design, and collaborative innovation that advances the future of mixed reality.
