About Us

We are designing and manufacturing an augmented reality headset.

What We Do

UCXR (University of Calgary Extended Reality) evolved from the original ARmed team, which was founded to design a fully custom augmented reality headset. As our vision expanded beyond a single device toward a full XR platform, we rebranded to UCXR to better reflect our broader focus on spatial computing, research, and innovation in mixed reality technologies.

We specialize in end-to-end development of AR systems ‚Äî from custom PCB design, optical engineering, and embedded systems to real-time visualization, SLAM, spatial mapping, and UI/UX optimization. Our work bridges hardware and software to create advanced tools for education, simulation, and professional training.

Our Mission Statement

To design, manufacture, and program next-generation Extended Reality (XR) technologies that redefine how users interact with digital content anchored in the physical world. Starting with applications in medical education, we aim to unlock new ways of learning, exploring, and building within mixed reality environments.

We are committed to engineering excellence, interdisciplinary collaboration, and iterative design to push the limits of what is possible in XR.
