Our Teams

Electrical

The Electrical Team brings the AR system to life, designing PCBs and integrating sensors, batteries, and essential electronics. Their precision engineering ensures seamless inter-process communication, optimizing performance and durability for an immersive AR experience.

Software

The Software Team develops the AR experience, building immersive interactions in game engines and optimizing real-time rendering. From sensor fusion, simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), and hand gesture recognition, they push the boundaries of spatial computing to make AR seamless and intelligent.

Mechanical

The Mechanical Team crafts the physical foundation of the AR glasses, from ergonomics to thermal management. From CAD/CAM to precision manufacturing, they focus on the visual clarity of optics, engineering lens configurations, displays, and light paths.

Modelling and Simulations

The Modelling and Simulations team develops anatomical models in 3D modelling software and integrates them into a game engine. By collaborating with the Software team, they implement a complete in-house solution for the working application.

Business

The Business Team is responsible for reaching out to sponsors and finding potential partners. They allocate funding, coordinate marketing events, and engage in conversations surrounding augmented reality and its uses.
