Our Team

Learn more about the teams and people that help AR-med thrive

Engineering Teams

The Engineering Team is responsible for identifying, researching, procuring, and integrating all components of the AR glasses, including sensors, batteries, and electrical elements. Their technical expertise and attention to detail should be reflected in the performance and durability in every aspect of the AR glasses' design and construction.

Within our Engineering Team, we have three major subteams, Mechanical, Electrical and Software. These teams work in tandem to create our headset.

Medical and Modelling Team

The Medical and Modelling Team leverages a variety of software tools, including Unity, Vuforia, and Blender, to breathe life into our immersive 3D models. Additionally, the team diligently validates model accuracy by conducting in-depth research, studying relevant research papers to ensure precision and authenticity in their work.
