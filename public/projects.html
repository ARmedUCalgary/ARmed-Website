Our Team

Learn more about the teams and people that help ARmed thrive

Electrical Team

The Electrical Team engineers the AR glasses' full electronic system, including PCB design, battery integration, sensor interfacing, and power management. They enable advanced features like haptic feedback, real-time sensor fusion, and high-efficiency communication between hardware layers, laying the foundation for responsive XR interactions.

Mechanical Team

The Mechanical Team focuses on the structural and manufacturing aspects of the glasses. They design lightweight, ergonomic enclosures, manage thermal control, and use advanced machining techniques to ensure precision fitting of critical components. Their work balances durability, aesthetics, and user comfort for extended XR sessions.

Software Team

The Software Team builds the intelligence behind the headset. They develop low-latency spatial mapping, SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) algorithms, and real-time data processing to anchor virtual objects accurately within the physical world. Their systems drive hand tracking, environment sensing, and intuitive haptic feedback to deliver a seamless XR experience.
