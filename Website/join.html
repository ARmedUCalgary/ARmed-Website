Our Teams

Engineering

The Engineering Team is responsible for identifying, researching, procuring, and integrating all components of the AR glasses, including sensors, batteries, and electrical elements. Their technical expertise and attention to detail should be reflected in the performance and durability in every aspect of the AR glasses' design and construction.

Medicine

The Medical Research and Modeling Team leverages a variety of software tools, including Unity, Vuforia, and Blender, to breathe life into their immersive 3D models. Additionally, the team diligently validates model accuracy by conducting in-depth research, studying relevant research papers to ensure precision and authenticity in their work.

Business

The Business Team is responsible for reaching out to sponsors and finding potential partners. This team is in charge of allocating funding. Marketing events are coordinated by this team to develop conversations around Augmented Reality and its uses.
